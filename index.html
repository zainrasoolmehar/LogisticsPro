Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our enterprise logistics solutions.

What makes LogisticsPro different from traditional courier services?

LogisticsPro offers enterprise-grade solutions with dedicated account management, real-time tracking, API integrations, and guaranteed 99.9% on-time delivery. Unlike traditional couriers, we provide scalable solutions tailored to your business needs with comprehensive support and transparent pricing.

How quickly can we integrate LogisticsPro with our existing systems?

Our technical team can complete most integrations within 48-72 hours. We provide comprehensive API documentation, dedicated technical support, and pre-built connectors for popular e-commerce and ERP platforms. Your dedicated account manager will coordinate the entire setup process.

What are your pricing models for enterprise clients?

We offer flexible pricing based on volume, service level, and geographic coverage. Our enterprise packages include volume discounts, predictable monthly billing, and no hidden fees. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific logistics requirements and shipping volumes.

Do you provide international shipping capabilities?

Yes, LogisticsPro offers comprehensive international shipping to over 200 countries with customs clearance support, duty management, and compliance assistance. Our global network ensures reliable delivery times and full tracking visibility for all international shipments.

What level of customer support do you provide?

All enterprise clients receive 24/7 customer support, a dedicated account manager, and priority technical assistance. Our support team includes logistics specialists who understand your business needs and can provide proactive solutions and real-time issue resolution.

How do you ensure package security and insurance coverage?

LogisticsPro provides comprehensive insurance coverage up to $100,000 per package, secure handling protocols, and chain-of-custody tracking. All shipments are monitored with GPS tracking, secure facilities, and background-checked personnel throughout the delivery process.
